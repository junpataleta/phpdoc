◆ TYPE_SYSTEM

string const core_question\local\bank\question_bank_helper::TYPE_SYSTEM = 'system'

The type of shared bank module that the system creates.

These are created in course restores when no target context can be found, and also for when a question category cannot be deleted safely due to questions being in use.
